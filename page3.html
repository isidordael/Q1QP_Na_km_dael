Conclusion

Our data set is a sample of the best-selling K-pop retail albums In 2022. In our data set, we had 50 samples of the entire population of the best-selling pop albums in 2022. The interesting fact about our report is that out of the 50 albums, 21 girl groups had the most albums sold in the K-pop industry. Which makes this the majority of the 4 different group categories combined. The results also tell us what the people prefer now. Since the majority of the sales are coming from Girl Groups, we can say that today is now the “Girl Group Age”. They captured the hearts of the people, which led them to be very profitable because they were in demand compared to the other group categories.
